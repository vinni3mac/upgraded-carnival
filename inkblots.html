Inkblots and Photographs

Coming Early 2026

"Inkblots and Photographs" represents Vincent Mano's deepest exploration into emotional storytelling through music. This upcoming album marks a significant evolution in his sound, embracing a slower, more introspective approach to electronic creation.

Each track will serve as an audio photograph - capturing raw emotional moments through lush synthesizers, delicate piano work, and Vincent's signature vulnerable vocal delivery.

The album draws inspiration from the contrast between fleeting digital moments and permanent emotional imprints, blending organic instrumentation with cutting-edge electronic production techniques.
